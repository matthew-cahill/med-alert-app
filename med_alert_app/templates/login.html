Why Remain Anonymous?

If you choose to remain anonymous:

  • Your report won't be associated with you.
  • You won't have access to view or edit your previous reports.
Anonymously Report an Incident

MedAlert receives complaints from current UVA students regarding healthcare practitioners within the UVA Health system who may have violated a regulation or law.

How it works:

  1. Submit a report with as much relevant information as you can. This includes the name of the practitioner, the time and location of the incident, a detailed summary of your concerns, and any related documents. The more information you provide, the better we can help you.
  2. A member of our staff will closely examine your report and open an investigation. If you submitted non-anonymously, your report will be marked “In Progress”. During this time, you can still make edits to your report by attaching new documents or providing new information.
  3. We will update you with a summary of our actions after the investigation, and any further steps that need to be taken on your part. If you submitted non-anonymously, your report will be marked “Resolved”. You are no longer able to edit your report at this time.
